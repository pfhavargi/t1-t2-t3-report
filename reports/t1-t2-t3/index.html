T1 CSAT trend, org-wide

% of CSAT survey respondents rating "Very Satisfied" or "Satisfied", across all T1 agents combined, by month.

Shown org-wide, not per-agent, because survey response volume is very low relative to case volume (7-10 responses per month across all of T1 combined, out of hundreds of cases handled) — too sparse for individual agent CSAT to be statistically meaningful. Treat month-to-month movement here as a weak signal, not a firm trend, until response volume improves.
